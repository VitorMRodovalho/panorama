# Trademark policy (draft)

*This page is a placeholder drafted 2026-04-17. Final policy will be reviewed
by counsel before first public release.*

## Name

The name **Panorama**, the `panorama.` wordmark, and any associated logos are
intended to be registered trademarks of the Panorama project.

## What you can do without asking

- Say "built on Panorama", "compatible with Panorama", "integrates with Panorama"
- Write blog posts, tutorials, videos that describe or demo Panorama
- Operate an internal deployment of Panorama at your organisation and call it
  "Panorama" there
- Fork the repository, modify it, and redistribute the modifications under AGPL,
  calling the fork **something else** (e.g., "Acme Asset Manager, a fork of
  Panorama")

## What you cannot do without permission

- Redistribute a modified version of Panorama under the name "Panorama"
  (change the name, honour the AGPL; explain the lineage clearly)
- Offer a commercial SaaS called "Panorama" that competes with the project's
  hosted offering
- Use the Panorama logo on your product's packaging, marketing, or app icons
- Register `panorama-*` domains or trademarks in the asset / fleet management
  space

## Community forks — naming hygiene

If you fork Panorama:

- Rename the product (e.g., "Acme Fleet")
- Remove / replace the Panorama logo
- Keep the AGPL notices intact
- Keep the attribution line in your About page or README: "Based on Panorama
  (https://panorama.vitormr.dev), AGPL-3.0-or-later"

## Commercial (Enterprise) edition

The Enterprise edition licence grants the customer the right to operate their
Panorama deployment under the Panorama name internally. It does NOT grant the
right to resell, rebrand, or sublicence Panorama.

## Questions

Email legal@vitormr.dev with trademark-specific questions. We aim to be
reasonable — the goal is preventing confusion in the market, not policing fair
use.
