# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**.

The full text is available at:
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

Reports of unacceptable conduct should go to
**conduct@vitormr.dev** (temporary contact while the project boots; will move
to a role address under the project's dedicated domain once external
contributions open).

Short summary:
- Be welcoming, respectful, and constructive
- Accept criticism gracefully; give it thoughtfully
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Maintainers enforce; repeated violations lead to removal

When reporting, include: who, what, when, where, and any relevant links.
Reports are confidential and we will not retaliate.
